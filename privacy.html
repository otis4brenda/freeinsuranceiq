Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Information We Collect

FreeInsuranceIQ does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data you enter into our calculators. All calculations run entirely in your browser. No calculator inputs are sent to our servers or any third party.

Google AdSense

We display advertising through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing activity. FreeInsuranceIQ does not control the content of these advertisements and does not share your personal information with Google for this purpose beyond standard web browsing activity. To learn more, visit Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site in aggregate. This includes pages visited, time spent on site, and general geographic location (city/state level). We do not collect names, email addresses, or personally identifying information through analytics. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

FreeInsuranceIQ itself does not set first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense and Google Analytics as described above. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

No Data Sales

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with insurance companies, brokers, lead generation services, or any other third parties. FreeInsuranceIQ is not an insurance lead generation site.

Children's Privacy

FreeInsuranceIQ is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us through our website. We aim to respond to privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
