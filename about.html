About FreeInsuranceIQ

We built the tool we wished existed when we were confused about insurance.

Why We Built This

Insurance is one of the most important financial decisions most people make — and one of the least understood. The existing resources are either written in impenetrable jargon, buried inside lead-generation funnels that require your phone number, or produced by the same companies trying to sell you a policy.

FreeInsuranceIQ exists to change that. We built a set of plain-language calculators that give you real numbers based on your real situation — with no email required, no sales pitch, and no data sold to insurance companies. You use the tool, get your answer, and walk away informed.

Our Principles

  • 🔒No personal data collected. Every calculation runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to our servers or any third party.
  • 🚫Not affiliated with any insurance company. We don't receive commissions, referral fees, or payments from any insurer. Our tools give you independent estimates.
  • 📊Based on real data. Our calculators use current 2026 industry benchmarks from sources like Genworth Cost of Care, LIMRA, J.D. Power, and federal government data (FPL guidelines, SSDI statistics).
  • 💡Plain language only. We explain every term, every number, and every result in everyday English — no acronyms without explanations, no jargon.
  • 🔄Kept current. Our content is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect current rates, laws, and market conditions.

How We Make Money

FreeInsuranceIQ displays Google AdSense advertisements. These are standard display ads — not insurance leads, not affiliate links to specific carriers. The ads shown are based on your browser's ad settings and are managed entirely by Google. We do not share your personal information to serve ads.

This advertising model lets us offer all calculators permanently free with no paywalls, no required email sign-ups, and no subscription fees. Ever.

Important Disclaimer

FreeInsuranceIQ's calculators provide estimates only. They are educational tools, not insurance advice. Actual insurance premiums, coverage amounts, and available products vary based on your individual circumstances, location, health history, and insurer.

Before purchasing any insurance policy, consult with a licensed insurance professional in your state. For health insurance, visit healthcare.gov for official enrollment and subsidy information.
